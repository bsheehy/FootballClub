Folks various peoples login details are on this email. Please be patient when using the system I’ve a few kinks to sort out and a few changes to improve performance. Any suggestions or feedback is welcome and any bugs you encounter just email me about them.

The first thing everyone should do is:
1.	Change your password
a.	To do this login with your username and password and click on the ‘My Account’ button at top of screen. You can change your password in there.
2.	Administer your team
a.	I’ve added most people as Team Admins for their respective teams. If I’ve missed you then send me an email and I’ll sort that out.
b.	If you are over a team you should add your Players as People then add these players to the team.
c.	NOTE: Always Search for a Person before adding a New One otherwise we will end up with duplicate Person records.
3.	Committee Members
a.	Committee Members can add Committee Minutes  through the system. 
b.	After completing your minutes you might want to print them off to keep a hard copy.

Every User has a Role which are Administrator, Committee Role, Club Role these roles are explained below:
•	Administrator – can do anything in the system include add\update User accounts,  add users to teams admins, add\edit lotto, add person membership
•	Committee Role - add\edit lotto, add person membership. Administer any teams where they have been added as a Team Admin.
•	Club Role – add documents and administer their teams.
